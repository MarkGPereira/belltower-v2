Welcome to The Bell Tower Estate

Nestled along the tranquil banks of Lake Openaka, Bell Tower Estate stands as an iconic Denville landmark with roots dating back to the 1700s. Originally a beloved fishing club in the early 1800s, the estate has been thoughtfully restored to its majestic, serene, and rustic elegance. Our mission is simple — to provide a breathtaking backdrop for your most meaningful celebrations and milestones. Blending timeless history with refined charm, Bell Tower Estate offers a setting unlike any other.
